Lasagna

Description

A recipe for lasagna with nothing homemade! Delicious? You tell me!

Ingredients

  • 1 store-bought frozen lasagna
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 2 tbs of olive oil
  • The juice of half a lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Open the box of your frozen lasagna.
  2. Put the lasagna in the oven, following the instructions on the box to know the temperature and how long for.
  3. While it's cooking, wash the salad.
  4. Prepare the vinaigrette. Whisk the olive oil, lemon juice, salt and pepper in a small bowl.
  5. Toss your salad in the bowl and mix until covered in vinaigrette.
  6. When the lasagna is cooked, it is ready to serve!
  7. This should not be really tasty, nor healthy, but now it is made, so eat it!
