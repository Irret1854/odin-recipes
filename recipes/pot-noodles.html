Pot noodles

Description

A cheap, but pretty disguting meal, for people who can't be bothered to cook

Ingredients

  • Your favorite brand of instant noodles
  • 1 pack of spices, included in the pack of noodles
  • 1 pack of dehydrated vegetables, included in the pack of noodles
  • Grated cheese (optionnal)

Steps

  1. Open the pack of noodles. Add boiling water up to the line.
  2. Add the spices. Discard the pack of dehydrated veggies, cause that's just gross.
  3. Cover the noodles for a few minutes.
  4. If you are feeling fancy, add grated cheese to the noodles once they are cooked. We do not recommend you do this.
  5. Relive your student days and enjoy!
