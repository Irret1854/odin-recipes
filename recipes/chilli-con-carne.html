Chilli Con Carne

Description

Terri's terrible Chilli con carne. Serve with rice if, unlike Terri, you know how to cook it.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2 carrots
  • 500g of ground beef
  • 1 can of concentrated tomato paste
  • 250g of cooked red beans
  • 1tbs of cumin
  • 1 tbs of paprika
  • 1 tbs of chilli flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cut the onion and carrots in small pieces. Cook until the onions are translucid
  2. Add the spices and cook while stirring for a couple of minutes. It should already smell really good!
  3. Add the meat. Cook on high heat until brown, then lower the heat
  4. Time to add your beans and tomato paste. Add water to cover. Add the chilli flakes. Let simmer for a while
  5. Once the water has reduced, serve with a garnish of coriander.
  6. Pretend you enjoy this dish!
